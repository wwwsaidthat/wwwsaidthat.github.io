Biography

Hi there! I'm Kaifang Yao (姚凯方), a 4th-year undergraduate student at the Software Institute, Nanjing University, advised by Prof. Tieke He and Prof. Jianhua Zhao.

My research interests lie in graph neural networks (GNNs), large language models (LLMs), and multi-agent systems. I'm passionate about developing efficient and scalable methods for learning on structured data and leveraging LLM-based agent architectures for real-world applications.

Currently, I'm working on graph representation learning, LLM-driven multi-agent collaboration, and exploring the intersection of structured data learning with foundation models.
